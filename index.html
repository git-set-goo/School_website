About Us

Hindi High School Ghatkopar with its high academic standard had carved for itself a place among the leading secondary schools in Maharastra state.

Highlights

The Headmaster of Hindi High School,Shri. I.D. Singh was conferred the National Award for Teachers in 1976 and was felicitated in may- 1977 by the Hon'ble President of India,Shri.B.D.Jatti for his valuable contribution in the field of education.

The school was given incentive grant by the government of maharastra for having a very high standard of education and the managment in 1979-80.

The school was awarded cleanest school Trophy by the Dy.Director of education in 1983-84.Its Hindi Primary section was adjudged the Best school among the private and Recognised Primary schools in Greater Mumbai by the education Department of Mumbai Muncipal Corporation in 1999-2000..
