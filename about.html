HVPS Hindi High School

Hindi high school,Ghatkopar with its high acedemic standard has carved for itself a place among the leading secondary schools in Maharastra state.

Highlight:

  1. The Headmaster of Hindi High School, Shri I. D. Singh was conferred the National Award for Teachers in 1976 and was feliciated in may-1977 by the Hon'ble president of India,shri B. D. jatti for his valuable contribution in the field of education.

  2. The school was given incentive grant by the government of maharastra for having a very high standard of education and the managment in 1979-80.

  3. The school was awarded cleanest school trophy by the Dy. Director of education in 1983-84.Its Hindi Primary section was adjudged the best school among the private and recognised primary schools in Greater Mumbai by the education department of Mumbai Muncipal corporation in 1999-2000.

  4. In march 2012,the school had the distinction of having 100% result in SSC Examination though more than 400 students appeared for the exam.

Vision:

Our Vision is to create a unity in trinity ( Management ,Teachers and Students ) to achieve our objectives to build a learning society covering all levels of populace of our motherland. We firmly believe that the economic disparity can be eradicated through education alone


Mission:

Our mission is education for one and all. We strive to blend quality teaching ,research employability and entrepreneurship. Although the school believes firmly in the importance of traditional values and standards, Hindi High School Ghatkopar is also committed to embracing the opportunities of modern educational methods and technological advances, preparing students to face today’s world with confidence and competence. Our aim is to encourage critical thinkers,problem anticipators and solvers; responsible global citizens who are prepared to achieve the highest standards in their subsequent education and career. The School experience should promote positive interpersonal relationship where students and staff members can work cooperatively with a partner,group or team to become caring sharing individuals,aware of the concern of others.
